SALT LAKE

CITY

Basic informations
  • Salt Lake City is the capital of Utah.
  • SLC has around 200,000 citizens, SLC is the core of SLC metropolitan area, which has a population of 1,160,000.
  • Utah is 13th-largest state of U.S. by area and 31st-most-populous state of U.S.
  • The city is situated within a larger metropolis known as the Salt Lake City-Ogden-Provo Combined Statistical Area.
  • The city is located in Salt Lake Valley.

Early history
  • The valley's first permanent settlements date to the arrival of the Latter-day Saints on July 24, 1847.
  • They were seeking a secluded area to safely practice their religion away from the violence and persecution they experienced in the East.
  • They found the broad valley empty of any human settlement.
  • Four dazs after arriving in the Salt Lake Valley, president of the church Brigham Young designated the building site for the Salt Lake Temple - famous Mormon and SLC landmark.
